# Leads Management

> Covers: Lead capture (manual, webhook, selfie analysis), lead assignment configuration, CRM actions, status pipeline, and conversion to Patient.
> Events emitted: `LeadCreated`, `LeadConverted`
> Events consumed: none (lead creation is a source event)

---

## Glossary

- **Lead**: A prospective patient who has not yet been converted to a Patient record.
- **Lead_Source**: `MANUAL` | `WEBHOOK` | `SELFIE_ANALYSIS` - see `shared/enums.md`.
- **Lead_Status**: `NEW` | `CONTACTED` | `QUALIFIED` | `CONVERTED` | `LOST` - see `shared/enums.md`.
- **Lead_Priority**: `LOW` | `MEDIUM` | `HIGH` | `URGENT` - see `shared/enums.md`.
- **Lead_Tag**: A free-text label attached to a Lead for categorization and filtering.
- **Lead_Action**: A logged interaction with a Lead including action type, optional status change, and content text.
- **Webhook_Source**: An external campaign system configured to push Lead data to the platform via HTTP POST. Configured by Organization_Admin only.
- **Field_Mapping**: A per-Webhook_Source configuration that maps incoming webhook payload fields to Lead profile fields.
- **Selfie_Analysis_Report**: The AI hair-loss assessment PDF generated by the Selfie Analysis web component.
- **Lead_Assignment_Mode**: An Organization-level setting configured by the Organization_Admin. Two values: `AUTO_ASSIGN` (default) and `MANUAL_ASSIGN`. Controls how incoming leads from API sources (webhook, selfie analysis) and Org Admin manual entries are assigned to clinics.
- **AUTO_ASSIGN**: The default mode. Leads are automatically assigned to the clinic determined by the source (selfie analysis selection, webhook field mapping, or Org Admin's explicit choice). Once a clinic is set, Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns staff.
- **MANUAL_ASSIGN**: The Organization_Admin reviews each incoming lead. The determined clinic (from source) is shown as a suggestion only. The Org Admin must manually confirm or change the clinic assignment. Until confirmed, the lead is Unassigned. Once a clinic is assigned, Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns staff.
- **Unassigned_Lead**: A Lead with `clinicId = null`. In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode, only occurs when no clinic can be determined from the source (e.g., webhook with no clinic in payload, Org Admin manual entry with no clinic selected). In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode, all incoming leads start as Unassigned until the Org Admin confirms a clinic. Visible to Organization_Admin only.
- **Lead_Distribution_Algorithm**: The pluggable mechanism by which incoming leads within a Clinic are distributed to active Staff members with lead access. The current implementation is Round_Robin (even rotation across eligible staff). The algorithm is defined as a separate, independently configurable component so it can be replaced without changing the lead assignment requirements.
- **Conversion**: The process of creating a Patient record from a Lead record, transferring applicable fields automatically.

## Lead Assignment Model

### AUTO_ASSIGN Mode (default)

```
Lead created
    │
    ├── Source: MANUAL by Clinic Staff
    │       └── Always assigned to Staff member's current Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │
    ├── Source: MANUAL by Organization_Admin
    │       ├── Clinic selected → assigned to that Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │       └── No Clinic selected → Unassigned_Lead
    │
    ├── Source: SELFIE_ANALYSIS
    │       ├── Single-clinic org → auto-assigned to that Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │       └── Multi-clinic org → visitor MUST select Clinic (mandatory) → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │           (Selfie Analysis NEVER produces Unassigned_Lead in AUTO_ASSIGN mode)
    │
    └── Source: WEBHOOK
            ├── Clinic in payload (via field mapping) → assigned to that Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
            └── No Clinic in payload → Unassigned_Lead
```

### MANUAL_ASSIGN Mode

```
Lead created
    │
    ├── Source: MANUAL by Clinic Staff
    │       └── Always assigned to Staff member's current Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │           (Clinic Staff manual leads are NOT affected by MANUAL_ASSIGN mode)
    │
    ├── Source: MANUAL by Organization_Admin
    │       └── Clinic shown as suggestion (if determinable) → Org Admin confirms/changes → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │           (If Org Admin does not confirm → Unassigned_Lead)
    │
    ├── Source: SELFIE_ANALYSIS
    │       └── Visitor-selected Clinic shown as suggestion to Org Admin → Org Admin confirms/changes → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
    │           (Until Org Admin confirms → Unassigned_Lead)
    │
    └── Source: WEBHOOK
            └── Clinic from payload shown as suggestion to Org Admin → Org Admin confirms/changes → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff
                (Until Org Admin confirms → Unassigned_Lead)
```

### Common Rules (both modes)

- **Clinic Staff manual leads** are always assigned to their own Clinic regardless of mode.
- **Once a Clinic is assigned**, Lead_Distribution_Algorithm distributes the lead to an active Staff member with lead access. Clinic_Admin is the fallback if no other eligible staff exists.
- **Invariant:** For any Lead L where `L.clinicId` is non-null, `L.assignedStaffId` MUST also be non-null.
- **Mode changes** affect only future leads. Existing leads are not retroactively reassigned.

## Lead Visibility Model

| Role | Sees |
|------|------|
| Any Clinic Staff (with lead access) | All leads in their Clinic, regardless of `assignedStaffId` |
| Clinic_Admin | All leads in their Clinic, regardless of `assignedStaffId` |
| Organization_Admin | All leads across all Clinics + Unassigned_Leads (`clinicId = null`) |

> **Note:** Per-staff lead visibility (staff seeing only their own assigned leads) is deferred to a future iteration.

## Requirements

### LM-1: Lead Assignment Mode Configuration

**User Story:** As an Organization_Admin, I want to configure how incoming leads are assigned to clinics so that I can control whether assignment is automatic or requires my review.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL provide an Organization-level `leadAssignmentMode` setting with two values: `AUTO_ASSIGN` and `MANUAL_ASSIGN`.
2. THE default value SHALL be `AUTO_ASSIGN`.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow Organization_Admins to change `leadAssignmentMode` at any time.
4. WHEN `leadAssignmentMode` is changed, THE Platform SHALL apply the new mode to all future leads only. Existing leads are not affected.
5. `leadAssignmentMode` applies to leads from: webhook sources, selfie analysis, and manual entries by Organization_Admin.
6. `leadAssignmentMode` does NOT apply to manual leads created by Clinic-level Staff - those are always assigned to the Staff member's current Clinic.
7. WHEN `leadAssignmentMode` is changed, THE Platform SHALL record the change in the Audit_Log.

#### Correctness Properties

- For any Lead L created after a mode change, L SHALL follow the assignment rules of the new mode.
- For any Lead L created before a mode change, L's assignment SHALL remain unchanged.

### LM-2: Lead Profile

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead create permission, I want to create and manage lead profiles so that the clinic can track all prospective patients in one place.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL store the following fields for each Lead: `name`, `age`, `gender`, `email`, `phone`, `createdAt`, `status`, `priority`, `tags[]`, `leadSource`, `sourceDetail` (page URL or campaign ID), `clinicId` (nullable for unassigned leads), `assignedStaffId` (nullable).
2. THE Platform SHALL allow duplicate Leads - the same email or phone MAY exist across multiple Lead records.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to update `status`, `priority`, and `tags` at any time.
4. WHEN a Lead is created via the Selfie_Analysis web component, THE Platform SHALL attach the selfie analysis questions, answers, and `Selfie_Analysis_Report` to the Lead profile. `Selfie_Analysis_Report` may be null if image capture failed or questions were incomplete - the Lead is still created and assigned.
5. WHEN a Lead is created, THE Platform SHALL set the initial `status` to `NEW`.
6. WHEN a Lead is created, THE Platform SHALL emit a `LeadCreated` event.
7. WHEN a Lead is created, THE Platform SHALL record the action in the Audit_Log.
8. Any Clinic Staff member with lead access SHALL see all leads belonging to their Clinic (`clinicId` matches their Clinic), regardless of which staff member is assigned.
9. Organization_Admins SHALL see all Leads across all Clinics within their Organization, including Unassigned_Leads (`clinicId = null`).

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Missing `name` field | `VALIDATION_ERROR` (field: `name`) |

#### Correctness Properties

- Two Lead records with identical email values SHALL both be creatable and retrievable without error.
- For any newly created Lead L: `L.status = NEW` immediately after creation.
- For any Clinic Staff member S in Clinic C with lead access: the Lead list query SHALL return all Leads where `clinicId = C`.
- For any Organization_Admin A: the Lead list query SHALL return all Leads in A's Organization including those with `clinicId = null`.
- For any Lead L where `L.clinicId` is non-null: `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL also be non-null.

### LM-3: Manual Lead Entry

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead create permission, I want to manually enter lead information so that I can capture prospective patients from phone calls, walk-ins, and other offline channels.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL provide a `createLead` mutation for Staff to manually create a Lead.
2. `name` is the only required field; all other fields are optional.
3. WHEN a Clinic-level Staff member creates a Lead manually, THE Platform SHALL automatically set `clinicId` to the Staff member's current Clinic and distribute the Lead via Lead_Distribution_Algorithm. This is not affected by `leadAssignmentMode`.
4. WHEN an Organization_Admin creates a Lead manually in `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode:
   - If a Clinic is selected → `clinicId` is set and Lead_Distribution_Algorithm distributes the Lead.
   - If no Clinic is selected → Lead is created as an Unassigned_Lead.
5. WHEN an Organization_Admin creates a Lead manually in `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode:
   - A Clinic selector is shown. Any pre-filled value is a suggestion only.
   - The Org Admin must explicitly confirm a Clinic to assign it.
   - If the Org Admin does not confirm a Clinic → Lead is created as an Unassigned_Lead.
6. WHEN a Lead is manually created, THE Platform SHALL set `leadSource` to `MANUAL`.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Missing `name` | `VALIDATION_ERROR` (field: `name`) |
| Unauthorized user attempting to create a lead | `FORBIDDEN` |

### LM-4: Webhook Lead Ingestion

**User Story:** As an Organization_Admin, I want to configure webhooks from external marketing campaigns so that leads are automatically captured in the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Organization_Admins (only) to configure one or more Webhook_Sources, each with a unique endpoint URL and a Field_Mapping.
2. THE Field_Mapping SHALL allow mapping each incoming webhook payload field to the corresponding Lead profile field, including an optional `clinicId` mapping.
3. WHEN a webhook payload is received, THE Platform SHALL parse it using the configured Field_Mapping and create a Lead record. The Platform SHALL NEVER reject lead creation from a valid webhook request due to staff availability.
4. WHEN a webhook payload is received, THE Platform SHALL set `leadSource` to `WEBHOOK` and `sourceDetail` to the campaign ID from the mapped field.
5. In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode:
   - If the payload contains a valid `clinicId` → Lead is assigned to that Clinic → Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff.
   - If no `clinicId` in payload → Lead is created as an Unassigned_Lead.
6. In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode:
   - The `clinicId` from the payload (if present) is stored as a suggestion on the Lead.
   - Lead is created as an Unassigned_Lead pending Org Admin confirmation.
7. IF a webhook payload is missing a required mapped field, THE Platform SHALL reject the payload, return an error response to the sender, and record the failure in the Audit_Log.
8. Webhook configuration (create, edit, delete Webhook_Sources) is restricted to Organization_Admins only.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | HTTP Status | Error Code |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Invalid API key | 401 | `WEBHOOK_INVALID_API_KEY` |
| Missing required mapped field | 422 | `WEBHOOK_MAPPING_ERROR` |
| Malformed JSON payload | 400 | `WEBHOOK_PAYLOAD_INVALID` |
| Webhook source not found | 404 | `NOT_FOUND` |
| Clinic_Admin attempting to configure webhooks | 403 | `FORBIDDEN` |

#### Correctness Properties

- For any webhook payload P with field mapping M, the Lead created from P SHALL have field values equal to `M(P)` for every mapped field.
- For any webhook payload P missing a required mapped field, the Lead creation SHALL fail and no Lead record SHALL be created.
- Webhook_Source configuration mutations SHALL fail for any actor who is not an Organization_Admin.
- In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode: a webhook lead with a valid `clinicId` SHALL have `assignedStaffId` set immediately after creation.
- In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode: all webhook leads SHALL be created as Unassigned_Leads regardless of `clinicId` in payload.

### LM-5: Selfie Analysis Web Component Lead Capture

**User Story:** As a prospective patient on a clinic's website or app, I want to complete a selfie-based hair analysis and select my preferred clinic so that I receive an instant assessment and the right clinic can follow up with me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL provide a Selfie_Analysis web component built with Stencil, embeddable on any Organization's website or app.
2. THE Selfie_Analysis web component is authenticated using the Organization's API key. There are no clinic-level API keys.
3. WHEN a visitor starts the Selfie_Analysis flow and the Organization has more than one active Clinic, THE Platform SHALL display a list of active Clinics for the visitor to select their preferred Clinic. Clinic selection is mandatory - the visitor cannot complete the flow without selecting a Clinic.
4. WHEN the Organization has exactly one active Clinic, THE Platform SHALL skip the clinic selection screen and auto-assign the lead to that Clinic.
5. WHEN a visitor completes the Selfie_Analysis flow, THE Platform SHALL capture: `name`, `age`, `gender`, `email`, `phone`.
6. THE Platform SHALL generate a `Selfie_Analysis_Report` and attach it to the Lead. The report may be null if image capture failed or questions were incomplete - the Lead is still created and assigned regardless.
7. In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode: the Lead is immediately assigned to the visitor-selected (or auto-assigned) Clinic and distributed via Lead_Distribution_Algorithm assigns Staff.
8. In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode: the visitor-selected Clinic is stored as a suggestion. The Lead is created as an Unassigned_Lead pending Org Admin confirmation.
9. WHEN the `Selfie_Analysis_Report` is generated, THE Platform SHALL offer the visitor the option to book an appointment via the Appointment Booking web component.
10. Setup and configuration of the Selfie_Analysis web component and its API key are restricted to Organization_Admins only.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Invalid or missing Organization API key | `WEBHOOK_INVALID_API_KEY` |
| Missing required visitor fields | `VALIDATION_ERROR` |
| Multi-clinic org: visitor attempts to complete flow without selecting a Clinic | `VALIDATION_ERROR` (field: `clinicId`) |

#### Correctness Properties

- In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode with a multi-clinic org: the visitor SHALL select a Clinic before completing the flow. The Lead SHALL be created with `clinicId` set to the selected Clinic and `assignedStaffId` set via Lead_Distribution_Algorithm.
- In `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode with a single-clinic org: the Lead SHALL be automatically assigned to that Clinic and `assignedStaffId` set via Lead_Distribution_Algorithm.
- In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode: all Selfie Analysis leads SHALL be created as Unassigned_Leads with the visitor-selected Clinic stored as a suggestion.
- For any Lead L created via Selfie_Analysis: `L.selfieAnalysisReport` may be null. The Lead is still created and assigned regardless.
- The Clinic list displayed to the visitor SHALL only include active Clinics belonging to the Organization.

### LM-6: Lead Assignment and Staff Distribution

**User Story:** As a Clinic_Admin, I want leads to be automatically distributed to my staff and to be able to reassign leads when needed so that no lead goes unattended.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Lead is assigned to a Clinic (at creation in `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode, or by Org Admin confirmation in `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode), THE Platform SHALL automatically assign the Lead to an active Staff member with lead access in that Clinic using Lead_Distribution_Algorithm.
2. THE Lead_Distribution_Algorithm SHALL cycle through all active Staff members with lead access in the Clinic in a consistent order, distributing leads evenly.
3. THE Platform SHALL NEVER reject lead creation from API sources (webhook, selfie analysis) due to staff availability. If no other eligible Staff member is found, the Lead SHALL be assigned to the Clinic_Admin as the fallback assignee.
4. WHEN an Organization_Admin assigns a Clinic to an Unassigned_Lead, THE Platform SHALL trigger Lead_Distribution_Algorithm within that Clinic.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow a Clinic_Admin or Staff member with reassign permission to manually reassign a Lead to another active Staff member within the same Clinic, provided the Lead's status is `NEW` or `LOST`.
6. THE Platform SHALL NOT allow regular Staff to reassign Leads in `CONTACTED`, `QUALIFIED`, or `CONVERTED` status.
7. WHEN a Lead is reassigned, THE Platform SHALL record the change in the Audit_Log including the previous and new assignee.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Reassigning a Lead not in `NEW` or `LOST` status (by non-admin) | `INVALID_LEAD_REASSIGNMENT` |
| Recipient Staff member is inactive | `RECIPIENT_INACTIVE` |
| Recipient Staff member belongs to a different Clinic | `FORBIDDEN` |
| Unauthorized user attempting manual lead creation | `FORBIDDEN` |

#### Correctness Properties

- For any Lead L assigned to Clinic C, `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL reference an active Staff member in Clinic C with lead access.
- The Lead_Distribution_Algorithm SHALL distribute leads evenly across eligible Staff members over time. The current implementation is Round_Robin. The algorithm is pluggable and can be replaced independently.
- After reassignment, `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL reference the new assignee and the Audit_Log SHALL record the previous assignee.

### LM-7: Unassigned Lead Management

**User Story:** As an Organization_Admin, I want to view and assign unassigned leads to clinics so that no lead is lost due to missing clinic assignment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

2. THE Platform SHALL allow Organization_Admins to assign a Clinic to any Unassigned_Lead. In `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode, the suggested Clinic (if present) is pre-filled but editable.
3. WHEN a Clinic is assigned to an Unassigned_Lead, THE Platform SHALL trigger Lead_Distribution_Algorithm within that Clinic.
4. Clinic-level Staff SHALL NOT see Unassigned_Leads.
5. WHEN an Unassigned_Lead is assigned a Clinic, THE Platform SHALL record the change in the Audit_Log.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Clinic-level Staff attempting to view Unassigned_Leads | `FORBIDDEN` |
| Assigning a Clinic from a different Organization | `FORBIDDEN` |

#### Correctness Properties

- For any Unassigned_Lead L, L SHALL NOT appear in any Clinic-level Staff member's lead list.
- After a Clinic is assigned to L, `L.clinicId` SHALL be non-null and `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL be set via Lead_Distribution_Algorithm.

### LM-8: Lead Actions and Notes

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead edit permission, I want to log interactions against a lead so that the team has a complete history of all communications.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to add Lead_Actions to a Lead record, each with: `actionType` (from dropdown), optional `statusChange`, and `content` text.
2. THE Platform SHALL support the following `actionType` values: `WHATSAPP`, `EMAIL`, `FACEBOOK_MESSAGE`, `PHONE_CALL`, `IN_PERSON_MEETING`, `OTHER`.
3. WHEN a Lead_Action is added, THE Platform SHALL record: `actionType`, `content`, `timestamp` (UTC), and the Staff member who added it.
4. THE Platform SHALL display all Lead_Actions for a Lead in reverse chronological order (newest first).
5. THE Platform SHALL preserve all Lead_Actions on the Lead record permanently. They are not deleted upon conversion.
6. WHEN a Lead is converted to a Patient, THE Platform SHALL link the Patient record to the originating Lead record so that Lead_Actions remain accessible via the Patient's history. Lead_Actions are referenced, not copied - no data is duplicated.
7. WHEN a Lead_Action is added, THE Platform SHALL record the action in the Audit_Log.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Invalid `actionType` value | `VALIDATION_ERROR` (field: `actionType`) |
| Lead not found | `LEAD_NOT_FOUND` |

#### Correctness Properties

- For any two Lead_Actions A1 and A2 on the same Lead where `A1.timestamp > A2.timestamp`, A1 SHALL appear before A2 in the list (newest first).
- For any Lead L converted to Patient P, all Lead_Actions on L SHALL remain accessible via P's linked lead reference. No Lead_Action data is duplicated or moved.

### LM-9: Lead Status and Priority Management

1. THE Platform SHALL support `Lead_Status` values: `NEW`, `CONTACTED`, `QUALIFIED`, `CONVERTED`, `LOST`.

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|

#### Correctness Properties

### LM-10: Lead Conversion to Patient

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead convert permission, I want to convert a qualified lead to a patient so that the clinic can begin formal treatment without re-entering their information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to convert a Lead to a Patient from the Lead profile page via a `convertLeadToPatient` mutation.
2. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL auto-populate the Patient profile fields from the Lead's data.
3. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL set the Lead's status to `CONVERTED`.
4. IF the Lead's `email` already exists as a Patient's `email` in the same Clinic, THE Platform SHALL block the conversion.
5. IF the Lead's `phone` already exists as a Patient's `phone` in the same Clinic, THE Platform SHALL block the conversion.
6. WHEN a Lead is successfully converted, THE Platform SHALL emit a `LeadConverted` event and record the conversion in the Audit_Log including the resulting Patient ID.
7. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL link the Patient record to the originating Lead so that Lead_Actions and the `Selfie_Analysis_Report` (if present) remain accessible via the Patient's history. No data is copied or moved.

#### Failure Cases

| Condition | Error Code |
|-----------|------------|
| Lead's email matches existing Patient email | `CONVERSION_DUPLICATE_EMAIL` |
| Lead's phone matches existing Patient phone | `CONVERSION_DUPLICATE_PHONE` |
| Lead already converted | `LEAD_ALREADY_CONVERTED` |
| Lead not found | `LEAD_NOT_FOUND` |

#### Correctness Properties

- For any Lead L converted to Patient P: every field present in L that maps to a Patient field SHALL be present and equal in P immediately after conversion.
- For any Lead L where `L.email` matches an existing Patient's email in the same Clinic: conversion SHALL fail and no Patient record SHALL be created.
- After conversion via LM-10: `L.status = CONVERTED`. Admin override via LM-9 AC-6 may revert this.

### LM-13: Lead Distribution Algorithm

> **Design note:** The Lead_Distribution_Algorithm is intentionally defined as a separate, pluggable component. Its assignment logic can be updated independently without changing the lead capture flow, assignment mode, or any other lead requirement. New rules can be added or reordered without modifying other lead requirements.

**User Story:** As a Clinic_Admin, I want the platform to automatically assign incoming leads to the most appropriate available staff member so that leads are handled promptly and fairly.

#### Distribution Trigger

WHEN a Lead is assigned to a Clinic (at creation in `AUTO_ASSIGN` mode, or upon Org Admin confirmation in `MANUAL_ASSIGN` mode), THE Platform SHALL invoke the Lead_Distribution_Algorithm to assign a Staff member to that Lead.

#### Assignment Rules (current implementation: Round_Robin)

The current implementation distributes leads evenly across eligible Staff members in rotation order. The algorithm SHALL be implemented as a separate, independently deployable service so that the distribution logic can be updated without modifying the lead capture or assignment flow.

| Priority | Rule |
|----------|------|
| 1 | Select the active Staff member with lead access in the Clinic who has received the fewest leads in the current rotation cycle (Round_Robin). |
| 2 | If all eligible Staff members have equal lead counts, select the next Staff member in the defined rotation order. |
| 3 | If no eligible Staff member is found (all inactive or no lead access), assign to the Clinic_Admin as the fallback. |

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Lead_Distribution_Algorithm SHALL run automatically whenever a Lead is assigned to a Clinic.
2. THE assignment result SHALL be stored on the Lead record as `assignedStaffId`.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow a Clinic_Admin or Staff member with reassign permission to manually override the assigned Staff member on any Lead in `NEW` or `LOST` status.
4. WHEN the assigned Staff member is manually overridden, THE Platform SHALL record the change in the Audit_Log including the previous and new assignee.
5. THE Lead_Distribution_Algorithm logic SHALL be implemented as a separate, independently deployable service so that distribution rules can be updated without modifying the lead capture flow.
6. THE Platform SHALL NEVER reject lead creation from API sources (webhook, selfie analysis) due to staff availability - the Clinic_Admin is always the fallback assignee.

#### Correctness Properties

- For any Lead L assigned to Clinic C, `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL reference an active Staff member in Clinic C with lead access.
- The Lead_Distribution_Algorithm SHALL distribute leads evenly across eligible Staff members over time. The current implementation is Round_Robin.
- After manual reassignment, `L.assignedStaffId` SHALL reference the new assignee and the Audit_Log SHALL record the previous assignee.
- `assignedStaffId` is always set when `clinicId` is set - a clinic-scoped lead without a staff assignment is not permitted.
