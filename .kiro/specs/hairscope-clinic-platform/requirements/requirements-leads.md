# Requirements Document — Leads Management

## Introduction

The Leads Management module provides a CRM for capturing, nurturing, and converting prospective patients (Leads) into active Patients. Leads can enter the system through three channels: manual staff entry, external campaign webhooks, and the Selfie Analysis web component embedded on a clinic website. The module supports lead status tracking, staff notes and actions, appointment booking, and conversion to Patient records.

---

## Glossary

Refer to the master requirements glossary for platform-wide terms. Additional terms specific to this module:

- **Lead**: A prospective patient who has not yet been converted to a Patient record.
- **Lead_Source**: The channel through which a Lead entered the system: Manual, Webhook, or Selfie_Analysis.
- **Lead_Status**: The current stage of a Lead in the nurturing pipeline: New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted, or Lost.
- **Lead_Priority**: A staff-assigned urgency indicator for a Lead.
- **Lead_Tag**: A free-text label attached to a Lead for categorization and filtering.
- **Lead_Action**: A logged interaction with a Lead, including action type, status change, and content text.
- **Webhook_Source**: An external campaign system configured to push Lead data to the platform via HTTP webhook.
- **Field_Mapping**: A per-Webhook_Source configuration that maps incoming webhook payload fields to Lead profile fields.
- **Selfie_Analysis_Report**: The AI hair-loss assessment PDF generated by the Selfie Analysis web component for a prospective patient.
- **Conversion**: The process of creating a Patient record from a Lead record, transferring applicable fields automatically.

## Requirements

### Requirement LM-1: Lead Profile

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead create permission, I want to create and manage lead profiles so that the clinic can track all prospective patients in one place.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL store the following fields for each Lead: Name, Age, Gender, Email, Phone, Date (of lead creation), Status, Priority, Tags, and Lead_Source.
2. THE Platform SHALL record the Lead_Source as either the page URL (for Selfie_Analysis web component leads) or the campaign ID (for Webhook leads) or "Manual" for staff-entered leads.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow duplicate Leads — the same email or phone number MAY exist across multiple Lead records.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to update the Status, Priority, and Tags of a Lead at any time.
5. WHEN a Lead is created via the Selfie_Analysis web component, THE Platform SHALL attach the selfie analysis questions, answers, and generated Selfie_Analysis_Report to the Lead profile.
6. THE Platform SHALL display the Selfie_Analysis_Report on the Lead profile page when present.
7. WHEN a Lead is created, THE Platform SHALL set the initial Status to New.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Duplicate allowance**: Two Lead records with identical email values SHALL both be creatable and retrievable without error.
- **Source accuracy**: For any Lead L created via the Selfie_Analysis web component, L.lead_source SHALL equal the URL of the page where the component was embedded.
- **Initial status**: For any newly created Lead L, L.status SHALL equal New immediately after creation.

---

### Requirement LM-2: Manual Lead Entry

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead create permission, I want to manually enter lead information so that I can capture prospective patients from phone calls, walk-ins, and other offline channels.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL provide a form for Staff to manually create a Lead with the fields defined in LM-1.
2. WHEN a Staff member submits the manual lead form, THE Platform SHALL require at minimum a Name field and return a validation error if it is missing.
3. WHEN a Lead is manually created, THE Platform SHALL set the Lead_Source to "Manual".
4. WHEN a Lead is created, THE Platform SHALL record the action in the Audit_Log.

---

### Requirement LM-3: Webhook Lead Ingestion

**User Story:** As a Clinic Admin, I want to configure webhooks from external marketing campaigns so that leads generated by those campaigns are automatically captured in the platform.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Clinic Admins to configure one or more Webhook_Sources, each with a unique endpoint URL and a Field_Mapping.
2. THE Field_Mapping SHALL allow the Admin to map each incoming webhook payload field to the corresponding Lead profile field.
3. WHEN a webhook payload is received at a configured endpoint, THE Platform SHALL parse the payload using the configured Field_Mapping and create a Lead record.
4. WHEN a webhook payload is received, THE Platform SHALL set the Lead_Source to the campaign ID extracted from the payload via the Field_Mapping.
5. IF a webhook payload is missing a required mapped field, THEN THE Platform SHALL reject the payload, return an error response to the sender, and record the failure in the Audit_Log.
6. THE Platform SHALL NOT assign webhook-created Leads to a specific Staff member — any Staff member with lead access may update them.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Field mapping round-trip**: For any webhook payload P with field mapping M, the Lead created from P SHALL have field values equal to M(P) for every mapped field.
- **Unmapped field rejection**: For any webhook payload P missing a field required by mapping M, the Lead creation SHALL fail and no Lead record SHALL be created.

---

### Requirement LM-4: Selfie Analysis Web Component Lead Capture

**User Story:** As a clinic website visitor, I want to complete a selfie-based hair analysis on the clinic website so that I receive an instant assessment and the clinic can follow up with me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL provide a Selfie_Analysis web component built with Stencil that can be embedded on any clinic website.
2. WHEN a visitor completes the Selfie_Analysis flow, THE Platform SHALL capture the visitor's Name, Age, Gender, Email, and Phone.
3. WHEN a visitor completes the Selfie_Analysis flow, THE Platform SHALL generate a Selfie_Analysis_Report and create a Lead record in the associated Clinic.
4. THE Platform SHALL attach the selfie analysis questions, answers, and Selfie_Analysis_Report to the created Lead record.
5. WHEN the Selfie_Analysis_Report is generated, THE Platform SHALL offer the visitor the option to book an appointment via the Appointment Booking web component.
6. THE Platform SHALL authenticate the Selfie_Analysis web component using a clinic-specific API key embedded at configuration time.
7. IF the clinic-specific API key is invalid or missing, THEN THE Platform SHALL reject all requests from that web component instance.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Lead creation on completion**: For every completed Selfie_Analysis flow, exactly one Lead record SHALL be created in the associated Clinic.
- **Report attachment**: For any Lead L created via the Selfie_Analysis web component, L SHALL have a non-null Selfie_Analysis_Report attached.

---

### Requirement LM-5: Lead Actions and Notes

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead edit permission, I want to log interactions and notes against a lead so that the team has a complete history of all communications.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to add Lead_Actions to a Lead record, each with: action type (selected from a dropdown), status change (optional), and content text.
2. THE Platform SHALL provide the following action types in the dropdown: WhatsApp, Email, Facebook Message, Phone Call, In-Person Meeting, and Other.
3. WHEN a Lead_Action is added, THE Platform SHALL record the action type, content text, timestamp, and the Staff member who added it.
4. THE Platform SHALL display all Lead_Actions for a Lead in chronological order on the Lead profile page.
5. THE Platform SHALL preserve all Lead_Actions until the Lead is converted to a Patient.
6. WHEN a Lead is converted to a Patient, THE Platform SHALL transfer all Lead_Actions to the Patient record for historical reference.
7. WHEN a Lead_Action is added, THE Platform SHALL record the action in the Audit_Log.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Chronological order**: For any two Lead_Actions A1 and A2 on the same Lead where A1.timestamp < A2.timestamp, A1 SHALL appear before A2 in the Lead_Actions list.
- **Action preservation**: For any Lead L with N Lead_Actions, after conversion to Patient P, P SHALL have at least N historical records corresponding to L's Lead_Actions.

---

### Requirement LM-6: Lead Status and Priority Management

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead edit permission, I want to update a lead's status and priority so that the team always knows where each prospect is in the sales pipeline.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL support the following Lead_Status values: New, Contacted, Qualified, Converted, and Lost.
2. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to change a Lead's Status to any value except Converted (which is set automatically on conversion).
3. WHEN a Lead's Status is changed to Lost, THE Platform SHALL require the Staff member to confirm the action.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to set and update a Lead's Priority.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to add and remove Tags from a Lead.
6. WHEN a Lead's Status, Priority, or Tags are changed, THE Platform SHALL record the change in the Audit_Log.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Converted status immutability**: After a Lead L is converted to a Patient, L.status SHALL equal Converted and SHALL NOT be changeable by Staff.
- **Status transition**: A Lead's Status SHALL only be set to Converted via the conversion process defined in LM-7.

---

### Requirement LM-7: Lead Conversion to Patient

**User Story:** As a Staff member with lead convert permission, I want to convert a qualified lead to a patient so that the clinic can begin formal treatment without re-entering their information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to convert a Lead to a Patient from the Lead profile page.
2. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL auto-populate the Patient profile fields with the corresponding Lead fields without requiring manual re-entry.
3. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL set the Lead's Status to Converted.
4. IF the Lead's Email already exists as a Patient's Email in the same Clinic, THEN THE Platform SHALL block the conversion and return a descriptive error indicating the duplicate.
5. IF the Lead's Phone already exists as a Patient's Phone in the same Clinic, THEN THE Platform SHALL block the conversion and return a descriptive error indicating the duplicate.
6. WHEN a Lead is successfully converted, THE Platform SHALL record the conversion in the Audit_Log, including the resulting Patient ID.
7. WHEN a Lead is converted, THE Platform SHALL transfer all Lead_Actions and the Selfie_Analysis_Report (if present) to the Patient record.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Conversion completeness**: For any Lead L converted to Patient P, every field present in L that maps to a Patient field SHALL be present and equal in P immediately after conversion.
- **Duplicate block**: For any Lead L where L.email matches an existing Patient's email in the same Clinic, the conversion SHALL fail and no Patient record SHALL be created.
- **Status finality**: After conversion, L.status = Converted and SHALL NOT be changed by any Staff action.

---

### Requirement LM-8: Lead Appointment Booking

**User Story:** As a Staff member with appointment create permission, I want to book an appointment directly from a lead's profile so that I can schedule consultations without switching modules.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow Staff to initiate an appointment booking from the Lead profile page.
2. WHEN booking an appointment from a Lead profile, THE Platform SHALL pre-populate the appointment form with the Lead's name and contact details.
3. WHEN an appointment is booked for a Lead, THE Platform SHALL send an email notification to the Lead's email address.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow a Lead to book their own appointment via the Selfie_Analysis web component after receiving their Selfie_Analysis_Report.

#### Correctness Properties

- **Pre-population accuracy**: For any appointment booked from Lead L, the appointment's contact fields SHALL equal L's corresponding fields at the time of booking.
